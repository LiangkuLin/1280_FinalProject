Important of health

Health is the center of our life. Without the good health, our life will really tough.

You cannot reach the following six areas if you do not have healthy body and enough energy. When you are not healthy, you are difficult to:

  1. Express your love and intimacy to your lover or family;
  2. Raise your children and support your family;
  3. have high productivity and great performance at work because losing concentration;
  4. Go travel because you do not have enough energy;
  5. Learn something if you have been struggling of your bad health;
  6. Contribute or help others as a result of incapable, and maybe you are the one who needs help;

Without good health, you have nothing!
