Muay Thai Competition

Rules & Equipments

To compete in the professional Muay Thai fight, several rules need to be satisfied. Firstly, fighter needs to be over 15 years old and at least 100 pounds. Secondly, all fighters need to wear gloves while competing. The fighter will be classified into weight divisions.

For Example,

  • Super Heavyweight 209+
  • Heavyweight 209
  • Cruiserweight 190
  • Light Heavyweight 175
  • Super Middleweight 168
  • Middleweight 160
  • Junior Middleweight 154
  • Welterweight 147
  • Junior Welterweight 140
  • Lightweight 135
  • Junior Lightweight 130
  • Featherweight 126
  • Junior Featherweight 122
  • Bantamweight 118
  • Junior Bantamweight 115
  • Flyweight 112
  • Junior Flyweight 108
  • Mini Flyweight 105
